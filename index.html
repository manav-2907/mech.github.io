MECH BLOWERS

We manufacture high performance Blowers.
Blower is equipment or a device which increases the velocity of air or gas when it is passed through equipped impellers.
They are mainly used for flow ofair/gas required for exhausting, aspirating, cooling, ventilating, conveying etc.
Mech holds most advanced manufacturing system with large number of CNC Machines Installed.
The Blower and other products are manufactured with optimum profile resulting in high efficiency ,high displacement,lower slippage and all products are energy efficient.
